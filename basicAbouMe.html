About Me

Keegan

Background: Hello, I’m Keegan. I live in Cincinnati, Ohio but am a staff member in a residence hall here on campus, so that’s where I currently am! My staff position is a Community Programmer! I work to make programming, bulletin boards, and promote inclusivity and education surrounding the theme of the Honors College.
This is my third year on campus and my second year working in a residence hall. I’m a little nervous to be here, especially with all my classes being online (essentially rendering me being here pointless…) but I’m hoping that being smart about what’s happening will keep me safe. With this sort of situation you have to rely not only on yourself, but others to do the right things.
Interests & Hobbies: I’ve never really had any consistent hobbies or interests, but I’m going to try and scrape the few I’ve maintained together here. I am generally a nerd, so that will reflect in the following bullet points! These are things that have also kept me sane during this extended time away from friends!
  • Sewing; I had to find a new job this summer, as my previous one let me go when COVID hit. So, I got a job at Joann fabrics! I bought a sewing machine off a coworker and started making my own masks, like many people did. I have a lot of supplies, but not a lot of time. So when things slow down and I’m on a roll again this semester, I’m looking forward to spending more time sewing again. I have a few cool fabrics I can’t wait to turn into masks and other small projects! Here is a link to my favorite strange fabric!
  • Listening to music; I think a lot of people would probably question whether or not this is actually a hobby, but this summer I spent a lot of time broadening my music taste. I really enjoyed this part of my summer, as it was something I was also able to share with my partner. It was a wonderful bonding activity! One of my favorite songs is by a Russian band called "Molchat Doma."
  • D&D; For those who aren’t familiar (which would surprise me in a COSC course), Dungeons and Dragons is a roleplay dice game. I only started playing this year, again when COVID hit, as my partner has a friend group that plays weekly, and they invited me to join back in March! Since then, I’ve really come to enjoy the game. The best part is definitely our DM. Dude’s a wizard when it comes to throwing curveball plotlines and fun enemies at us. I joined half-way through the campaign, but I can’t wait to see it through and start a whole new character!
Dreams & Goals: To finish this degree! It really took a lot for me to finish last year, so I’m really trying to push through and complete these last couple of semesters. The morale was certainly low at the end of last semester, but I have high hopes for this semester. I think being on campus and being grounded in my position as a Community Programmer will help keep me focused and push through while doing my best this semester.
I’ve also been working on changing my style, especially in clothing. I’ve always wanted to adopt a goth fashion, and that’s finally coming into being alongside my newly discovered love of the goth subculture (of course that mainly entails the music), but I’m really excited to see where I end up next!
